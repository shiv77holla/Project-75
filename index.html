CONSOLES

The console screen is a part of almost every coding or webpage system. The consoles in both coding platforms and webpages are the same.Both of them have the same function.


Function of Consoles:

Consoles are used to detect errors, values of certain variables, they allow the programmer to see the hidden errors that wont normally appear on the screen or ones that seem to be fine, but do not behave how tohey are programmed to. They can also be used as a tool to know values of variables at the specific moment.

Opening a Console:

To open a console, follow the step by step instructions below:
  • Right click on the webpage.
  • Click on "Inspect Element" on the menu that just appeared.
  • Once you see a seperate screen of your right side, click on the "console" option in the top right place.
  • Great you just opened your console!
